Google would say aspiration in English and चाह, इच्छा in Hindi, but the Akankshaa I know is 90% desires and 10% aspiration.


Lifestory:

I was born to middle-class engineer parents in a small-town Balangir in western Odisha.

My parents always stressed upon the importance of knowing and speaking my mother tongue, so I learned how to speak, read and write(in that order)as I grew up.

My mother tongue is Odia, and as mentioned before, I can proudly say that I speak, read and write Odia.

My parents moved to Noida(NCR) in Uttar Pradesh when I was 2. Hence, I was primarily brought up in Noida.
